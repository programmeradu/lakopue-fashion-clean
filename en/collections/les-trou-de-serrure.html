THE KEYHOLE

Originally designed by Elsa Schiaparelli herself as a clasp on a handbag in order to bring an aura of mystery to this everyday object, the keyhole is an iconic code of the Maison. Daniel Roseberry continues to use the emblem throughout his collections on bags, bijoux, shoes, buttons and intricate embroideries.
