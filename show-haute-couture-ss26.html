Show Notes

A Dialogue of Light & Shadow

For Spring–Summer 2026, the Maison explores the contrast between armour and air. Structured tailoring frames ethereal tulles; rigid gold surfaces float above liquid silk. The keyhole motif appears as both aperture and jewel — a threshold between seen and unseen.

The palette moves from deep onyx and antique gold to lacquered scarlet and porcelain ivory, echoing the glow of Accra at dusk. Each piece is hand-finished in the Maison’s ateliers, celebrating the dialogue between couture technique and contemporary attitude.
